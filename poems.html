ARVOLAN POETRY

soft words for soft hearts

a small beginning
there are days when your steps feel too small to count...
untangled
not every knot inside you needs to loosen today...
the gentleness you deserve
you spent years trying to earn softness...
on the days you feel behind
there is no deadline you were meant to meet...
what stays
the world taught you to measure your value...
soft rebellions
choosing rest when you're tired is still a victory...
if today feels heavy
you don’t have to solve your whole life from here...
still becoming
you are allowed to outgrow older versions of yourself...
the quiet strength
strength is not always loud...
you are allowed to change
you don’t owe anyone the person you used to be...
